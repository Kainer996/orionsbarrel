Meet the Locals

The colorful characters who call Orion's Barrel home. Each with their own story, dreams, and reasons for finding themselves among the stars.

Mary

Owner & Proprietor

The heart and soul of Orion's Barrel, Mary is a graceful alien woman whose warm presence makes everyone feel welcome in the vastness of space. With her keen business sense and genuine care for her patrons, she's turned this remote station into a true home away from home. Mary has an uncanny ability to remember everyone's favorite drink and always knows exactly what someone needs‚Äîwhether it's a strong cocktail, a listening ear, or just a place to belong among the stars.

Carl

Deep Space Miner

A gentle reptilian with scales that shimmer like emeralds under the station lights. Carl spends his days working the asteroid belts, but his heart belongs to a dream he's never seen‚Äîowning a loyal dog from Earth. He's saved up enough credits for three dogs, but first he needs to figure out how to get to the blue planet. Until then, he's the friendliest face at the bar, always ready with a story from the mines and a genuine smile.

Walter

Station Elder

The oldest regular at the Barrel, Walter has seen three different owners run this place and helped build half the station with his own weathered hands. His purple-tinged skin tells the story of decades spent in the cosmic radiation of deep space. Walter doesn't say much, but when he does speak, everyone listens. He's got wisdom earned through countless jumps, close calls, and quiet nights watching the stars wheel by.

If you need something that's not on the menu‚Äîor the manifest‚ÄîScally's your guy. He operates in the gray spaces between legal and illegal, always with one eye on the exit and the other on the next deal. Don't ask too many questions, and you'll get along just fine.

Dick

Truth Seeker

Dick's the kind of guy who thinks he's figured out what "they" don't want you to know‚Äîand he's not shy about sharing it. With his perpetual scowl and condescending tone, he'll lecture anyone within earshot about government cover-ups, false flag operations, and how the space lanes are really controlled by shadowy corporate overlords. Most patrons have learned to nod politely and slowly back away when Dick starts talking.
